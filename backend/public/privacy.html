Privacy policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Heaven’s Boutique (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use our website, iOS application, and related services (the “Services”).

Information we collect

Account and profile. When you register or shop, we collect information you provide, such as name, email address, shipping address, and order history.

Payments. Payment card data is processed by our payment partners (e.g. Stripe). We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

Device and usage. We may collect device identifiers, push notification tokens if you opt in, and diagnostic data to operate and improve the Services.

Local storage. The iOS app may store cart contents on your device (e.g. via UserDefaults) so your bag persists between sessions.

How we use information

  • Process and fulfill orders, payments, and returns;
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries;
  • Send transactional messages (order confirmations, shipping updates) and, where you allow it, marketing or promotional notices;
  • Detect fraud, abuse, and technical issues;
  • Comply with legal obligations.

Sharing

We share information with service providers who assist us (hosting, payments, email delivery, analytics as applicable), subject to contractual protections. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.

Retention

We retain information as long as needed to provide the Services, meet legal requirements, and resolve disputes. You may request deletion of your account through the app; some records may be retained where the law requires.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect personal information. No method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

Children

The Services are not directed to children under 13 (or the age required in your region). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Your choices

You can update account information in the app, adjust notification permissions in iOS Settings, and contact us to exercise rights available in your jurisdiction (such as access or deletion, where applicable).

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. We will post the new version on this page and update the “Last updated” date.
